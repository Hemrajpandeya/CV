Hem Raj Pandeya

I'm Hem Raj Pandeya, a dedicated mechanical engineer driven by innovation, holding a degree from Tribhuvan University, Pulchowk Campus.

In my capstone project, I worked on Eagle Ray, a meticulously designed blended-wing body drone aimed at safeguarding endangered Snow Leopards from poaching. My involvement extended to optimizing the Eagle Ray's aerodynamics, enhancing its efficiency.

Furthermore, I possess extensive experience with drones, utilizing them to collect real-time data from various sensors and generating 3D maps based on parameters like temperature and humidity.

I've contributed my expertise at Prokura Innovations and the National Innovation Centre, where I deepened my knowledge in CAD, simulations, machinery and tool utilization, as well as fabrication processes. These experiences also enhanced my ability to collaborate within a team.

I possess a strong interest in control systems and find myself captivated by various related topics. Outside of my professional pursuits, I enjoy constructing and piloting RC planes. Additionally, I am an avid reader of novels, enthusiastic about hiking, and passionate about cycling.
